Notre processus

Five steps. Clear deliverables. Zero surprises.

A process designed for couples living thousands of miles away who need absolute certainty everything is under control.

01

Discovery call

Duration · 30 min · Free

We meet the couple, listen to vision, tentative dates, budget and religious priorities. We answer all your questions.

  • Zoom or WhatsApp Video call
  • Initial questionnaire by email
  • Available in Arabic, Spanish or English

Deliverable

Conversation summary + suggested tier proposal within 48h.

02

Proposal & venue selection

Duration · 2–3 weeks

We present 3 halal-friendly venues with detailed budget, exclusive photos, custom mood board and real availability.

  • Virtual or in-person site visit
  • Side-by-side comparison
  • Rate negotiation with hotel

Deliverable

3 venues + initial budget + mood board. Contract signed with chosen venue.

03

Design & vendors

Duration · 4–8 weeks

We lock catering, decor, photography, imam, entertainment and production. Each vendor pre-validated halal by us.

  • Final mood board with renders
  • Virtual tasting menu available
  • Each vendor approved by the couple

Deliverable

Full list of signed vendors + master event timeline.

04

Guest logistics

Duration · 8–12 weeks before

Group lodging block, airport transfers, RSVP, event website and welcome kits with Islamic touches.

  • Bilingual event website
  • Guest visa advice
  • Welcome kit with dates, rose water and spiritual guide

Deliverable

Live event site + confirmed guest list + kits ready for delivery.

05

Event day(s)

Duration · 1–3 days

Dedicated on-site team, trilingual coordinator, walkie-talkies, timelines kept to the minute. You just get married.

  • 2–4 coordinators depending on package
  • Halal-aware medical emergency team
  • Wrap-up with farewell brunch next day

Deliverable

Your wedding. Plus all photos, videos and final deliverables within 30 days.
