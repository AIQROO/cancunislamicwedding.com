About us

Un equipo musulmán-mexicano dedicado exclusivamente a tu nikah en el Caribe.

We were born in Cancún in 2019 with one idea: that no Muslim couple should have to choose between celebrating in a dream destination and living their faith without compromise. Today we're specialists in Islamic destination weddings — and only that.

Our story

We started with an impossible wedding. Today they're our specialty.

In 2019, a Saudi-Canadian couple asked for our help. They wanted a nikah in Cancún, but no planner could give them certainty around halal catering, prayer space or a local imam. We had to build that network from scratch.

That wedding became our founding case. We went from planning all kinds of events, to focusing exclusively on Muslim couples. Today we've planned over 120 weddings and work with families from 14 countries.

What sets us apart isn't what we do — it's what we no longer do. We don't take events where we can't 100% guarantee your faith is respected.

Values

Three non-negotiable principles.

i.

Halal with no asterisks

Certified kitchen, strict separation of utensils and traceable suppliers. If we can't guarantee it, it doesn't enter the menu.

ii.

Deep respect

Privacy for women, prayer space, cultural sensitivity. Faith isn't decor — it's the structure of the event.

iii.

Latin warmth

Mexican hospitality at the service of your wedding. Attention to detail, kept timelines, and that Caribbean magic only found here.
