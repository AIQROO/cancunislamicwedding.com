3. Services offered

We provide planning, coordination and production services for Islamic weddings (nikah, walima, mehndi and related events) in Cancún, Playa del Carmen, Tulum and other destinations in southeast Mexico. Services may include: Islamic officiation by an imam, certified halal catering, venue selection, decor, atmosphere, transport, lodging, photo and video, and overall day-of coordination.

The specific scope of each service is detailed case-by-case in the proposal signed by both parties.

5. Booking

Booking is formalised by (i) signing the services proposal and (ii) paying the corresponding deposit. Until both are completed, the date is not reserved and remains available to other clients.

6. Payments

  • Deposit: 30% of total upon signing to reserve the date.
  • Second payment: 40% of total no later than sixty (60) calendar days before the event.
  • Final payment: remaining 30% no later than fifteen (15) calendar days before the event.

Payments may be made by domestic or international bank transfer, in Mexican pesos (MXN) or US dollars (USD) at the exchange rate agreed in the proposal. Late payment entitles the provider to suspend services without liability.

7. Cancellations & rescheduling

Client cancellation

  • More than 120 days before the event: 100% of the deposit is retained (non-refundable).
  • Between 119 and 60 days before: deposit plus 50% of payments made to date are retained.
  • Less than 60 days before: 100% of payments made are retained.

These retentions cover work already performed, vendor reservations and venue penalties that can no longer be reversed.

Rescheduling

A reschedule within the following twelve (12) months, subject to venue and vendor availability, does not trigger a penalty, except for unrecoverable expenses already paid. Later reschedules are treated as a new booking.

Cancellation by the provider

If the provider is unable to perform for reasons attributable to it, the client will be refunded the full amount paid, without additional penalty.
