Real stories

Two weddings. Two continents. One promise.

Aisha & Yusuf · Toronto → Cancún · Feb 2026

Un nikah frente al mar, con familia desde tres países.

Aisha is Palestinian-Canadian; Yusuf, Lebanese-American. They wanted a middle ground for their families and chose Cancún. 65 guests, private resort walima, mihrab-arched decor and certified halal catering.

«The most magical part was seeing my cousins from Ramallah dancing with the Mexican catering family. You don't plan that — but you let it happen.»

Layla & Karim · Londres → Tulum · Nov 2025

Tres días — mehndi, nikah y walima — en una hacienda con arcos.

A Pakistani-Moroccan wedding in 3-day format. 130 guests from 9 countries. Private-garden mehndi with henna and Andalusian music, sunrise nikah under a palm arch, evening walima with DJ and cinematic production.

«Three families, three traditions, three days. And we never had to explain twice what halal means.»
