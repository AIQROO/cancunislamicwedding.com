Curated halal hospitality

Halal certified, not halal-friendly.

The difference between 'halal-friendly' and halal certified is the difference between good intentions and a guarantee. We operate on the second level.

We work exclusively with suppliers verified by Muslim Friendly Halal Certification, an allied certifier of our group. Catering, tableware, space separation, alcohol-free zones, salat times, qibla direction, wudu areas — every element of your wedding is reviewed by Muslims who understand the detail.

Our closeness to the Muslim community of Quintana Roo gives us something money can't buy: the local network. When you say 'Cancun Islamic Wedding', doors open that stay closed to others.
